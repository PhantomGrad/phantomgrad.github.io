Large Language Models & Generative AI

Department of Computer Science & Information Systems · BITS Pilani

An advanced course covering the foundations and frontiers of large language models — transformer architecture, pre-training, instruction tuning, RLHF, prompt engineering, evaluation, and responsible deployment. Includes hands-on projects using state-of-the-art open and closed models.

Community & Outreach

AI for Rural Education

We have conducted research on the impact of large language models on K-12 education in rural India, working with student volunteers to understand how AI tools are being adopted — and where gaps remain — in under-resourced educational settings. This work was published in Scientific Reports (2025) and was supported by the WISE–IIE Higher Education Consortium grant.

Read the paper ↗

AI for River & Climate Conservation

In partnership with The Nature Conservancy, we applied AI and ML methods to river basin management — quantifying the effects of land use and climate change on hydrology, and identifying degraded riparian corridors for targeted conservation efforts.
