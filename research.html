Research

Publications, funded projects, research themes, and academic collaborators.

Active Themes

What We're Working On

AI Agents & LLMs

Evaluation, benchmarking, and applications — negotiations, planning, reasoning, and real-world deployments.

AI for Education

Intelligent tutoring systems, automated grading, LLM-assisted teaching, and studying AI adoption in classrooms.

AI for Software Engineering

Code generation, test synthesis, type inference, loop invariant generation, and formal verification with LLMs.

Reinforcement Learning & LLM Training

Policy optimization, LoRA fine-tuning, evolutionary meta-solvers, and hybrid supervised-RL frameworks.

Mechanistic Interpretability

Understanding polysemanticity, circuits, and internal representations within transformer-based models.

Multi-Modal Foundation Models

Vision transformers, time-series foundation models, and tabular model interpretability.

AI for Science & Environment

Machine learning for hydrological modeling, wetland assessment, and climate-driven river analysis.

Distributed Systems & Edge Computing

Geo-distributed analytics, federated learning, WAN-aware query optimization, and edge intelligence.

Data Mining & Parallel Computing

Scalable clustering algorithms (DBSCAN, OPTICS, SLINK) on commodity clusters and multicore systems.

Seeking Collaborators

Areas we are actively exploring and looking for research partners in:

AI Safety & Jailbreaking

Adversarial robustness, prompt injection, and safety alignment of LLMs.

Multi-lingual & Cultural AI

LLM performance and bias across languages, scripts, and cultural contexts.

Games & Sports AI

Applying LLMs and RL to game-playing, strategy, and sports analytics.

Bias in LLMs

Measuring and mitigating cultural, linguistic, and length biases in language models.

Completed
