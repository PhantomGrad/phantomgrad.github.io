Phantom Grad Lab

Advancing AI through rigorous research, responsible deployment, and real-world impact.

We are an AI research group at BITS Pilani working at the intersection of large language models, intelligent systems, and real-world applications. Our work spans foundational research on LLM evaluation, interpretability, and training — and applied projects in education, software engineering, environmental science, and beyond. We partner with industry, government agencies, and NGOs to translate research into impact.

Research Focus

What We Work On

AI Agents & LLMs

Evaluation, benchmarking, and applications of large language models in real-world settings.

AI for Education

Intelligent tutoring, automated assessment, and understanding LLM impact on learning.

AI for Software Engineering

Code generation, test synthesis, type inference, and formal verification with LLMs.

Mechanistic Interpretability

Understanding internal representations and circuits within transformer models.

Multi-Modal Foundation Models

Vision, time series, and tabular foundation models and their adaptation.

RL & LLM Training

Policy optimization, fine-tuning strategies, and hybrid learning frameworks.
