Lab Director

Dr. Dhruv Kumar

Assistant Professor, Computer Science & Information Systems · BITS Pilani

Dhruv Kumar is an Assistant Professor at BITS Pilani, leading PhantomGrad. His research spans large language models, AI for education, mechanistic interpretability, reinforcement learning, and distributed systems. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, and has previously worked at Microsoft Research and Google Cloud. He has received multiple Best Paper Awards and Teaching Excellence Awards.
