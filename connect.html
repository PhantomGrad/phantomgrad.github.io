Who We're Looking For

Work With Us

If any of the following describes you, we would love to hear from you. Reach out with a brief introduction and what you have in mind.

Researchers & PhD Students

Looking to collaborate on research papers, joint projects, or research visits in AI, LLMs, or related areas.

Industry Partners

Companies seeking research-focused AI consultancy — evaluation frameworks, benchmarking, model analysis, or applied research.

Government & Policy

Agencies looking for evidence-based AI research to inform policy, regulation, or public sector deployments.

Social Impact Organizations

NGOs and nonprofits working on education, environment, healthcare, or rural development who want to leverage AI responsibly.

BITS Pilani Students

Undergraduate, Master's, and PhD students at BITS Pilani interested in research projects or thesis work with the lab.

Funding Bodies

Foundations and agencies interested in sponsoring research on AI evaluation, education, or real-world AI deployment.
